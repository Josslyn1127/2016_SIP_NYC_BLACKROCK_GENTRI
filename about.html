Who are we?

We are coding rockstars! Seven weeks ago, we walked into the Blackrock building to begin our first day of Girls Who Code, a summer computer science intensive program. Throughout our time here, we have explored several languages including Python, Scratch, C++, Arduino, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This is our final project.

Meet Jessica Asamoah

Jess is an 11th grader who resides in Georgia. She was originally born in Kumasi, Ghana and migrated to the states at the tender age of 2 years old. Her hobbies include making art, listening to music, and her newly found love, coding!

Once she was accepted into the GWC Summer Immersion program in New York, she got the chance to travel to the city for the first time ever in her life. Her favorite parts about the city include the lights, the views, and the diversity of the population. Her favorite food is fettuccine alfredo with shrimp and her spirit animal is an elephant. In future she hopes to travel the world, make money, and be happy! :)
