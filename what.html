WHAT IS GENTRIFICATION?

Gentrification is a controversial trend in urban environments characterized by the arrival of wealthier people into existing poor neighborhoods. Gentrification has been depicted as both a liberator and an annihilator of impoverished districts.

This GIF Showcses the past and the present.Gentrification is a controversial trend in urban environments characterized by the arrival of wealthier people into existing poor neighborhoods. Gentrification has been depicted as both a liberator and an annihilator of impoverished districts. There are many attractive aspects of gentrification. It adds infrastructure, increases property value, and generates economic activity in underprivileged communities. Schools improve, the median household income rises, and the neighborhoods become much safer to live in. (http://www.pbs.org/pov/flagwars/what-is-gentrification/) But these changes rarely benefit those who were already living in these communities.
